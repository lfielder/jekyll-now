Welcome!

My name is Lane Fielder. I am a Lead UX Designer at Rackspace. I have had the opportunity to work across many different types of interfaces including ecommerce, public facing control surfaces, and internal systems. I have led other designers and also worked as a solo designer with a development team. I enjoy collaborating with product managers and ensuring we are focused on both business goals and user goals.

To me, design is about complex problem solving. I specialize in breaking down difficult problems and coming up with testable and iterative solutions.

If you are looking for a Lead UX Designer, please feel free to contact me.

My design process is adaptable, but at a high level I like to ensure that stakeholders and project teammates are aware of what to expect and how each phase informs and leads tothe next phase. I bring along dev, product, and business stakeholders with this entire process.
