My name is Lane Fielder. I am a UX Designer at Rackspace. I have had the opportunity to work across many different types of interfaces including ecommerce, public facing control surfaces, and internal systems. I have led other designers and also worked as a solo designer with a development team. I enjoy collaborating with product managers and ensuring we are focused on both business goals and user goals.
